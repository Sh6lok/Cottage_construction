Our materials

Neolith

Zaha Stone

Composite material with the texture of natural stone, resistant to scratches, stains, and UV rays. Zaha Stone is inspired by the design of the renowned architect Zaha Hadid, giving your home a modern and stylish look. Ideal for both indoor and outdoor use, including, countertops, and walls.

Dekton

Laurent

High-quality composite stone inspired by natural Port Laurent marble. Laurent features unique textures and deep tones, resistant to scratches, stains, and temperature changes. Perfect for countertops, walls, and floors, adding a modern and elegant look.

Porcelanosa

Marmi China

Snow-white marble with delicate gray veins, adding refinement and nobility to your home. Marmi China is known for its clean white background, creating a bright and spacious look in any room. Popular among discerning clients, ideal for bathrooms, hallways, and fireplaces.

Marazzi

Calacatta Gold Marble

This elite marble with exquisite gold veining will add luxury and elegance to your interior. Calacatta Gold is known for its unique pattern, which gives each piece its own personality. It is ideal for use in bathrooms, kitchens and floors, creating a unique atmosphere.

Cosentino

Silestone Eternal Statuario

Quartz composite with vivid veins reminiscent of Statuario marble. This material is highly resistant to stains, scratches, and damage, making it an ideal choice for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic areas. Eternal Statuario ensures durability and style for many years.
